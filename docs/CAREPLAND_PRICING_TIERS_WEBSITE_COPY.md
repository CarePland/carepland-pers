# CarePland Pricing Tiers Website Copy

Draft source for the public website pricing section. Keep this patient-facing and avoid technical metering language such as tokens, credits, model usage, or context units.

## Positioning

CarePland plans increase the amount of continuity support, automation, and care coordination the system provides. The premium experience should feel calmer and more automatic, not like core appointment memory is being artificially restricted.

## Tier 1: Free

Purpose: Introduce CarePland's appointment continuity loop.

Best for: Light or occasional appointment tracking.

Includes:

- Basic appointment tracking.
- Limited manual CarePrep generations.
- Limited document uploads, OCR processing, and calendar imports.
- Self-service help.

Behavior:

- The user manually generates CarePrep when needed.
- Automatic CarePrep generation is not included.

## Tier 2: Active Use

Purpose: Support active healthcare management without full automation.

Best for: Chronic care, frequent appointments, or specialist-heavy care.

Includes:

- Larger manual CarePrep generation allowance.
- Expanded document uploads, OCR processing, and calendar imports.
- CarePland assistant and chat support access.
- Increased historical/context depth.

Behavior:

- The user intentionally generates CarePrep when needed.
- CarePrep remains primarily manual.

## Tier 3: Premium Individual

Purpose: Deliver proactive continuity support for one person.

Best for: People who want CarePland to quietly do more preparation work.

Includes:

- Automatic CarePrep for medical appointments.
- Smart reminders and proactive preparation workflows.
- Generous upload and import allowances.
- Enhanced support responsiveness.
- Reduced manual effort across workflows.

Behavior:

- CarePrep can automatically generate after appointment creation, appointment import, or medical appointment detection.
- CarePland should avoid automatically preparing non-medical appointments when possible.

## Tier 4: Group

Purpose: Support coordination across multiple Care VIPs.

Best for: Families, caregivers, or groups managing care for others.

Includes:

- Multiple Care VIP profiles.
- Automatic CarePrep across multiple people.
- Shared continuity workflows.
- Group-oriented coordination support.
- Highest upload and import allowances.
- Most generous support access.

Behavior:

- Intended for people coordinating care across more than one Care VIP.
- Supports longitudinal continuity across multiple individuals.

## Early Access

Purpose: Distinguish early adopters from later paid subscribers.

Best for: Early adopters who should retain broad CarePland Personal access while billing is still being shaped.

Includes:

- Current Group-level functionality.
- Multiple Care VIP profiles.
- Automatic CarePrep across multiple people.
- Highest upload and import allowances.
- Most generous support access.

Behavior:

- Functionally mirrors the current full-access multi-person tier.
- Should be assigned intentionally so early adopters can later be differentiated from paid subscribers.

## Trial And Upgrade Notes

Free users may temporarily experience selected higher-tier automation features to demonstrate the value of automatic continuity, proactive preparation, and reduced cognitive effort.

The emotional goal is: "The system quietly helps me stay prepared."
